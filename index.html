How to Use

  1. Open the page in your browser
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Click Start Detection to activate the AI
  4. Show your ASL signs clearly to the camera
  5. Click Interpret Word to see results
  6. Click Clear Buffer to start over

Pro Tips

  • Use good lighting for best accuracy
  • Keep hand centered in frame
  • Hold each sign for 1-2 seconds
  • Plain background works best
  • Maintain consistent distance from camera

Troubleshooting

Camera Not Working?

Ensure no other application is using your webcam and refresh the page.

Low Accuracy?

Improve lighting, use a plain background, and hold signs steadily.

Model Not Loading?

Check your internet connection and try refreshing the browser.

Slow Performance?

Close other browser tabs and ensure your device meets minimum requirements.
